    HOA and Kappes-Miller Common Area Designation

    I think this may sums up what we have been trying to communicate to the HOA and Kappes-Miller for the last 3 years.

    Washington County Information

    The Washington County record CCR BOOK 1060, PAGE 566 specifies unit 43 and unit 44 have the same amount of Limited Common Area. The HOA and Kappes-Miller have not produced anything that contradicts The Washington County record CCR BOOK 1060, PAGE 566-571 are attached.

    We are unit 43. This arial view shows that units 43 and 44 do not have the same amount of Limited Common Areas. The HOA and Kappes-Miller for the last 3 years have maintained the that the 40% of our backyard outlined in yellow is Common Area that we are no allow to use

    Section 3.3 Unit Designation table, page 569, shows unit 43 is 1,252 sq.ft of floor space and with 1.8765 Percentage of Interest in General Common Elements. That equals 1,252 X 1.8765 = 2,350 sq. feet ownership in the General Common area. My measurements agree with this number.

    General Common Elements page 570 states:

    Each unit shall be entitled to percentage ownership in the general common elements as set forth above in Section 3.3 and shall be liable for the general common expenses in the game proportion.

    I have spent time over 3 years collecting and sharing information with HOA and Kappes-Miller hoping to resolve this problem.

    HOA Response

    I asked the HOA to join Mary and me to look at and discuss the problems. A board member accepted and we had a productive discussion ending with the board member saying it is Common Area. I asked why, the board member did not have an answer.

    I asked this question to the HOA over the years. The few times there was response, the answer was the same
